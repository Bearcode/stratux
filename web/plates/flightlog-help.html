The FlightLog page provides a history of flights recorded by your system. Note that flight logging requires the Stratux have a GPS connected and locked. You also need to enable "Record Replay Logs" and set the Flight Log Level to at least "Logbook" in the "Settings" panel.

The current state of you device is shown at the top - Connected in green or Disconectedin red.

The playback mode is also shown at the top. If a playback is under way, the system will display - Simulation Mode in blue.
